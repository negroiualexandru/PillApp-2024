### *Personal Medication & Appointment Tracker*

[![Android](https://img.shields.io/badge/Android-3DDC84?style=flat&logo=android&logoColor=white)](https://developer.android.com/) [![Java](https://img.shields.io/badge/Java-007396?style=flat&logo=java&logoColor=white)](https://www.java.com/) [![SQLite](https://img.shields.io/badge/SQLite-003B57?style=flat&logo=sqlite&logoColor=white)](https://www.sqlite.org/) [![XML](https://img.shields.io/badge/XML-FFA500?style=flat&logo=xml&logoColor=white)](https://www.w3.org/XML/)

**PillApp** is a native Android application designed to replace scattered physical notes with a centralized "Virtual Pillbox." [cite_start]Built with **Java** and **SQLite**, it helps users manage their health regimen by tracking daily medication intake and scheduling doctor appointments in one secure interface[cite: 35].

---

## üöÄ Key Features

* **Virtual Pillbox:** Easily add, edit, or remove medications from your digital inventory, keeping your prescription list up to date.
* **Intake History:** Tracks exactly when you last took a specific medication (e.g., for pain management), helping prevent accidental double-dosing and ensuring safe usage intervals[cite: 35].
* **Appointment Manager:** dedicated section to log upcoming doctor visits, ensuring you never miss a consultation.
* **Unified Health Dashboard:** Consolidates medication schedules and medical appointments into a single view, eliminating the need for multiple physical papers or sticky notes.
